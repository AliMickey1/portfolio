Who Am I?

Hi I'm Aliceson Phillips

Welcome to My Digital Workshop

In my digital workshop, I wear many hats (sometimes all at once, which can get a bit tricky) to create a seamless and enchanting experience for you.

As a teacher, I am your guide, leading you through the intricacies of technology and design with clear, insightful instructions. Think of me as your tech-savvy tour guide, minus the funny hat and megaphone.

As an instructional technology coach, I am the innovator, constantly integrating the latest tech tools to enhance your learning experience. I make complex concepts as easy to grasp as your morning coffee (or tea, if that’s your thing).

As a graphic designer, I am the artist, crafting visually stunning elements that capture your attention and convey information effectively. Every design is a piece of art, thoughtfully created to make you go “Wow!” instead of “Huh?”

As a video editor, I am the storyteller, bringing dynamic, multimedia content to life. I weave together visuals and narratives to create captivating lessons that are both informative and entertaining. Think of me as the Spielberg of educational videos, but with fewer explosions.

Finally, as a web developer, I am the architect, building the foundation and structure of this digital workshop. I ensure that it is responsive, user-friendly, and seamlessly functional, providing you with a smooth and enjoyable experience. If websites were houses, mine would be the cozy, well-organized one with great Wi-Fi.

Together, these roles create a holistic, interactive, and enriching environment where learning and creativity thrive. So, grab a seat, and let’s dive into the world of tech and design—no hard hats required!
